Local Radar

Searching for local information shouldn't always require a map. People often just want to search for information nearby their house or other place.

You can embed your own local radar. You start by configuring a webmap with layers for each topic, and then embedding this web component into your website.

Explore Albuquerque Schools

<hub-radar 
webmap="be5753126b744043ba42e76a5381432e">
</hub-radar>
<script 
  src='https://unpkg.com/@esri/radar@0.1.12/dist/radar.js'>
</script>
