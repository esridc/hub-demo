Simple Open Data

Developers that want to find open data may just want to see a simple table of data with links to download.

Now you can embed your own open data table. Just enter the Hub site and optionally a query or limit. 

<hub-content-table 
    search="*" 
    limit="350" 
    site="opendata.victoria.ca">
</hub-content-table>
